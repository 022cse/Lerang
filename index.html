Food

Enjoy traditional African cuisine, including grilled meats, stews, and exotic fruits. Here are some of the must-try dishes:

Seswaa and Pap

A traditional dish consisting of slow-cooked beef served with a soft maize meal called pap.

Rice and Chicken

A simple but delicious dish of rice and flavorful chicken, often served with a side of vegetables or salad.

Sorghum and Beef

A nutritious dish combining sorghum grains with tender beef cooked in a rich, flavorful sauce.
